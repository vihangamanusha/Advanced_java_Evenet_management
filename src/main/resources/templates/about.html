ABOUT EVENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

This Event Management System is developed for the Faculty of Technology, University of Ruhuna. It allows internal and external users to easily book venues, manage events, and track booking status online.

OUR PURPOSE

Our goal is to simplify the event booking process, reduce manual paperwork, and provide a transparent system for managing venue reservations and approvals.
